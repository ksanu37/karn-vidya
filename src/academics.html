Academics

Academic Philosophy

At Karn Vidya Vihar, we believe in nurturing curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning. Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong academic foundation while encouraging creativity, collaboration, and holistic development.

Curriculum & Key Subjects

Science

Hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

Mathematics

Building logical reasoning and problem-solving skills from basics to advanced concepts.

Languages

English, Hindi, and Sanskrit with a focus on communication and literature.

Social Studies

Understanding history, geography, civics, and the world around us.

Computer Science

Digital literacy, coding, and technology integration for the 21st century.

Arts & Sports

Visual arts, music, dance, and a wide range of sports for all-round growth.

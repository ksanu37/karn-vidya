About Us

Karn Vidya Vihar is a leading CBSE school that has been providing quality education to students for over 20 years. Our mission is to empower young minds with quality education and comprehensive development through our innovative No Bag, No Tiffin policy.

We offer a wide range of academic and extracurricular activities to ensure that our students are well-rounded and prepared for the future. Our experienced educators are dedicated to providing a holistic learning experience that prepares our students for success in their academic and personal lives.
